Notepad Online
  • About Page

Notepad Online is a online "Sticky Note" application. It can be good for studying, doing homework, or just marking down all the tasks for the day. It is very simple and easy to use. You can press on the create new button located in the top right corner. A box will pop up telling you to write a title and body. In there you can put whatever you want, and after you're done, you press save and your note pops up on the screen. Whenever you finish the task you made a note for you can delete each Sticky Note. We believe this app will make it easier for everyone to be able to complete their tasks or homework.
