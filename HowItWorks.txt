How NotePad Online Works:

  NotePad Online is an online "Sticky Note" styled webpage used to keep track of daily notes the user wishes to add.
  This is designed to help organization and to be useful for the unorganized.
  Juat click "Create New", enter a Title and the body of the note. Hit save and the data will be posted on the main page.
  If you want to clear a note, just hit "Delete" and it will remove that note. IF you want to clear all of them, hit "Delete all" at the top of the page.
